About Zoom Be 3 👑

Zoom Be 3 is the thrilling conclusion to the cooperative alien blob platformer series — the most expansive adventure, the hardest puzzles, and brand-new enemy types. Guide both the green and pink blobs through the final lab labyrinth using their unique complementary abilities. What sets it apart is the integration of combat elements into core puzzle-platforming, demanding strategic thinking and quick reflexes simultaneously.

Game Features

  • The most complex cooperative 2-Player gameplay in the series
  • New enemy types and hazards to outsmart
  • Dozens of extreme, multi-step environmental puzzles
  • Two unique alien characters with complementary skills
  • Advanced teamwork, strategy, and coordinated action

How to Play 🎮

Two players share the keyboard. Player 1: WASD — Player 2: Arrow Keys. Flawless coordination is essential. Keep one blob safe while the other solves — synchronize jumps and button presses to beat the hardest levels in the series!
