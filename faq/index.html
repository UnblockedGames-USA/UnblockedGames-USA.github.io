❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is Unblocked Games USA?

Unblocked Games USA is a free browser-based gaming portal featuring hundreds of games playable directly in your browser — no downloads, no sign-ups, no plugins required. Our games work on school networks, public Wi-Fi, and anywhere else you want to play.

Are the games really free?

Yes — every game on this site is 100% free to play. We will never charge you or ask for payment details. Just click and play instantly.

Do I need to create an account?

No account is needed. All games are accessible directly from the browser with no registration, email, or personal information required.

Why are some games "unblocked"?

Many schools and workplaces block popular gaming sites. Our games are hosted on GitHub Pages which is often whitelisted. If a game doesn't load, please check that your browser allows iframes or JavaScript.

Which browsers are supported?

All modern browsers are supported — Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox, Microsoft Edge, Safari, and Opera. For the best experience we recommend using the latest version of Google Chrome or Firefox.

Can I suggest a game?

We love suggestions! Please use our Contact page to send game requests. We add new games regularly.

Are the games safe for kids?

We aim to keep all content school-friendly and age-appropriate. Games marked in the Kids category are specifically selected for younger audiences. However, parental guidance is always recommended for online content.

Why does a game not load?

Please try: (1) Refreshing the page, (2) Clearing browser cache, (3) Trying a different browser, (4) Disabling any ad-blocker extensions that may interfere with iframes.

How often are new games added?

We add new games frequently. Check back regularly or browse the categories to discover new titles!

Do you collect personal data?

We do not collect personally identifiable information. Please see our Privacy Policy for full details.
